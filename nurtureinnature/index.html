Welcome to Nurture in Nature

We are a Not-for-Profit organization made up of parents who love the outdoors. Like many who now enjoy life outside, we started off as city folk who weren't really sure how to get ourselves out into the big wide world. We wondered if there could be benefits to letting our kids play in the mud and get a bit dirty, and decided to find out for ourselves.

We know how difficult it can be to take the first literal and figurative steps out your door. That's why we created Nurture in Nature. We wanted to give you a starting point, a place where you could come to find community and figure out where you can even go.

We serve the Greater Ottawa area in Ontario, Canada, and function as a sort of online database of locations that you can visit. This area of the province is absolutely beautiful and rich with culture and filled to the brim with life outdoors. There is so much to do, and there is so much beautiful nature to experience.

We look forward to seeing you on the trails and hearing what a great time you have with your kids, pets, and other adult friends all over Ottawa.

See you out there!
